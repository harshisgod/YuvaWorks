Milestone-Based Payments

Why Use Milestones?

Milestone-based payments break down the project into manageable chunks, ensuring both parties are protected throughout the process.

Benefits for Freelancers:

  • ✓ Steady cash flow throughout the project
  • ✓ Clear deliverables and expectations
  • ✓ Protection against scope creep

Benefits for Clients:

  • ✓ Pay only for completed and approved work
  • ✓ Track project progress easily
  • ✓ Better budget management
